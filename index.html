Section Purpose
Posts A section where users can create, edit, and manage posts made.
Media A section where users can upload, organize, and manage media files.
Pages A section where users can create, edit, and manage pages.
Comments A section where users can manage comments made on posts and pages.
Appearance A section where users can customize the look and feel of their website.
Plugins A section where users can install, activate, and manage plugins to extend the functionality of their website.
Users A section where users can manage user accounts and permissions.
Tools A section where users can access various tools for managing their website.
Settings A section where users can configure various settings for their website.
